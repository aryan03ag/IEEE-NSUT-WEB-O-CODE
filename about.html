ABOUT US

Hi, there! We are a team of 3 highly motivated, highly enthusiastic gamers Aryan, Pranav and Nischay, all of us having our roots in the Indian Capital City of New Delhi.

We are delighted to announce our latest venture, APN GAMING, created by us with an aim to spread the culture of E-SPORTS in India, where it is still in the nascent stage.

We have planned to start out with creating a platform where gamers from all over the world can share their opinions, raise their queries, know about the lastest trending games,
know about the best equipment available commercially, write and read blogs and can be made aware about the upcoming touraments and their eligibility criteria.

We have plans to eventually expand the platform and incorporate state of the art features such as voice and video chat, streaming options among many others.
